WHAT IS BAC
  • Located at Pune,India in a stand-out, modern complex is the BLING AVIATION CLUB (BAC), the home to around 75 commercial pilot and flight instructor trainees per year.
    • Being a DGCA approved flying school, BAC is one the most trusted place to conquer your dreams of being a commercial/private pilot.

    BAC is proud to bring to you an eLearning course designed and developed by Industry professionals and subject matter experts. This online course carries the same passion and quality promise that BAC is well known for.



    The BLING AVIATON CLUB(BAC), has some exclusive benefits that help our students progress smoothly through their chosen courses. Flight training from an international airport is the obvious one, for multi crew coordination IFR training in particular, but a wide range of experience is also gained from operating out of 500m runways as is the case at our exclusive satellite airfield which is just five minutes away.It adds a third base to the mix and provides quick access to mountain flying terrain.
